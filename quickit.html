Quickit

In collaboration with my teammates, we redefined Quickit website, developed a B2B strategy for Linkedin and created an introductionary video.

About the project

Github Repository

Quickit is a small startup company that developed a software to assist the staff in the restaurant industry. Together with my teammates we redesigned the website, according to the target audience - businesses, and we have created a SoMe campaign called Quick2it, together with an informative motion graphic video, that could be used to bring awareness to Quickit in the restaurant industry.

For this project we have used Asana, to keep track of our tasks over time and see what needs to be done and what has been done. It is also helpful for each teammate to assign themselves to a task, add comments on the task and to update regularly for easier time management.


My role in this project was primarily to implement the website according to our Figma prototype. In order to make our solution more dynamic and easily updateable for the client, I have created a database using restdb.io that holds all the information required. The data is fetched in Javascript and then displayed on the website.

We have used four different research methods - Observation, Survey, Interview and Desk Research.

We optimised Quickits website so the information is communicated in a clear manner and is informative to the potential customers. From our research done on the current website, we concluded that the website would benefit from a call-to-action element in the form of a contact and book-a-meeting page in order to get leads and generate new clients.
